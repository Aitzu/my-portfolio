About Me

Born and raised in Taipei, Taiwan where I graduated from National Taiwan University, the #1 university in Taiwan, in business administration.

I moved to Beijing in 2010 for my first job in Nokia as an account manager for Nokia’s digital music and ebook services. Then I joined Sina Weibo (aka the Chinese Twitter) to experience working in a Chinese company and Skyscanner as their mobile marketing manager.

For (slightly) better air quality, in 2015 I moved to Shanghai where I joined Bon App, a F&B startup. I was in charge of marketing strategy, digital marketing, community, and event management. I turned its annual event from money-spending to a major money-making source with ABInbev, Pernod Ricard as sponsors and 1.5 million(RMB) average net profit.

I met my lovely husband in Shanghai. In 2018 we decided to take our 2 Chinese dogs and move to Melbourne. At the same time I decided to expand my skillsets by attending Coder Academy’s fast track coding bootcamp where I found my passion to programming. Now I’m focus on being a better programmer every single day.
